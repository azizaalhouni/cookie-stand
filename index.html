  • The freshest ingredients from the Skagit Valley are brought together in an exciting display of taste and talent at the superb Two Salmon Cafe. The featured Buffet incorporates several live-action cooking stations and a level of food quality and presentation not currently experienced in Washington State. Guests may choose from the best of Pacific Northwest’s finest seafood, Italian and American cuisines, offered throughout the week, or enjoy an exquisite brunch on Sunday. Adding to the buffet decadence is the succulent Prime Rib and Crab Buffet on Sunday evenings allowing guests a taste of the Northwest’s spectacular dining experience.
